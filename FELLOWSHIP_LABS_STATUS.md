## Quality Standards

All labs follow these standards (based on completed labs):

### Structure
1. **Lab Overview** - Course, code, title, duration, difficulty, objectives
2. **Ethical Warning** - For exploitation labs
3. **Lab Introduction** - Context and learning objectives
4. **Lab Setup** - Prerequisites, environment setup, installation instructions
5. **Exercises** - Multiple parts with progressive difficulty
6. **Code Examples** - Properly formatted with syntax highlighting
7. **Security Analysis** - Vulnerability identification and impact
8. **Mitigation** - Secure coding practices and remediation
9. **Verification** - Testing procedures and checklists
10. **Cleanup** - Environment reset instructions
11. **Submission Requirements** - Deliverables and grading criteria
12. **Additional Resources** - Further reading and practice platforms

### Content Quality
- **Comprehensive:** 3,000-5,000 words per lab
- **Practical:** Hands-on exercises with real tools
- **Progressive:** Beginner to advanced difficulty
- **Professional:** Industry-standard practices
- **Ethical:** Clear warnings and legal considerations
- **Well-formatted:** Proper markdown, code blocks, tables
